# PayAPI Market — Pricing

Machine-readable pricing for AI agents and crawlers.

## Plans

### Free
- Price: $0 / month
- Platform fee: 3% per successful payment
- Provider keeps: 97%
- Listings: unlimited
- Includes: marketplace listing, MCP Registry sync, per-request USDC payouts, analytics dashboard

### Featured
- Price: $49 / month
- Platform fee: 2.5% per successful payment
- Provider keeps: 97.5%
- Listings: unlimited
- Includes: everything in Free, gold badge, top placement in search and category filters, priority in agent tool manifests, priority support

## Buyer-side
There are no buyer-side subscriptions. AI agents pay per request in USDC via the x402 protocol. Settlement is on Base, ~400ms finality.

## Currency
- Settlement: USDC on Base
- Provider payouts: direct to provider wallet, every successful call
